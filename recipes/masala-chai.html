Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai is an Indian beverage popular throughout South Asia.

Chai is made by brewing black tea in milk, water, sugar and lastly adding aromatic herbs and spices.

Ingredients (Servings: 2)

  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, chopped
  • 4 whole cloves
  • ½ cinnamon stick
  • 5 pods green cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 pod black cardamom, split open
  • 2 black tea bags
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
